Christopher Nolan's Timeline

Explore the key milestones in Nolan's career from his early works to his blockbuster hits.

  • 1998: Following - Nolan's debut feature film.
  • 2000: Memento - A nonlinear psychological thriller.
  • 2002: Insomnia - A tense murder investigation in Alaska.
  • 2005: Batman Begins - The start of the acclaimed Dark Knight Trilogy.
  • 2006: The Prestige - Two magicians locked in deadly rivalry.
  • 2008: The Dark Knight - Batman battles the Joker in Gotham City.
  • 2010: Inception - A groundbreaking exploration of dreams.
  • 2012: The Dark Knight Rises - Batman's final confrontation with Bane.
  • 2014: Interstellar - A space odyssey beyond imagination.
  • 2017: Dunkirk - A war epic with innovative narrative structure.
  • 2020: Tenet - A mind-twisting action thriller with time inversion.
  • 2023: Oppenheimer - The atomic bomb and the moral dilemmas surrounding it.
